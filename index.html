How Smoothly Works

Simplifying heathy eating


Make your own smoothie

Use the sunburst to choose ingredients. Tap on the five different categories; fruits, liquids, nuts and seeds, herbs and spices, vegetables. Touch and hold on a specific ingredient and then drag to the left to set the amount of it. Did you change your mind about the amount? Donâ€™t worry you can change it in the table by touching the amount of the ingredient and then drag left or right to decrease or increase respectively. Name your smoothie, upload a picture of it and save it.

Use the already saved recipes

Tap on the search icon to see the already save recipes. In that step, you can choose ingredients in the sunburst to filter the recipes and see the recipes that only contain it. Tap on a nutrition category to order the recipes based on that nutrition. The brighter the color of the box the bigger the percentage.

Benefit from your personal RDI

In the start page, enter your gender, age, weight, height and intensity of daily exercise in order to use your daily recommended intake of nutrition. Compare ingredients in the bar chart and see how healthy is a smoothie for you, and how much it fulfills your daily needs in energy, protein, fat and carbonates.

About the project

Smoothly. Your online smoothy maker


Smoothly is a web-based application designed preliminary for tablets and helps you make your own (healthy) smoothie by visualizing ingredients and nutrition. In the application you can either make your own smoothie or choose one from a list and edit it.

However, not all the smoothies are healthy; the health of a smoothie depends on its ingredients. Smoothies with a lot of sweet fruits or sweetener can increase the carbonates without knowing it. Through Smoothly you can really know the exact nutrition that your smoothie contain and make one according to your personal daily needs.

Smoothly it is a fun and easy tool to use. There are two main visualizations. A sunburst where user only by touching can easily choose ingredients from a big variety of fruits, vegetables, dairy products, seeds, herbs and spices and then specify the amount of it. The second visualization work in parallel with the first one and provides information about the nutrition of the smoothie that the user has made so far in a stacked bar graph.

Moreover, the chosen ingredients are added to a table where the user can change the amount of them or delete them, knowing the same time the impact of those changes in the smoothie nutrition. When the recipe is done it can be saved for the next time. Finally, the user can choose from the already saved smoothie recipes. He can filter them based on ingredients, order them based on his preferable nutrition or edit the portion of the ingredients.
